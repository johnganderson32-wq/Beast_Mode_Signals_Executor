SIGNAL QUALITY FIELDS (visible in [webhook] log line):
  instrument          NQ or GC
  direction           bullish / bearish
  price               Entry close price (market order will fill near this)
  stop                Stop loss price
  tp1                 TP1 = entry +/- 0.5R
  target              Target = entry +/- 1R
  rDist               Inside zone range in points (your risk unit)
  compression         Inside zone as % of prior zone — lower = more compressed
  entryHour           ET hour of entry (should match Set F hours)
  macroTransition     Which macro zones formed the Inside (e.g. M2_to_M3)
  h4ZoneId            "pending" until Pine v3 adds H4 zone classification

SECTION 7 — TRADE ANALYTICS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

All analytics are computed live from the in-memory trade store and served
through the dashboard. No scripts to run — just open the browser.

  http://localhost:3100  →  Analytics tab

AVAILABLE METRICS:
  Win Rate          % of closed trades that hit TP1 or TARGET
  Expectancy        Average R-multiple per closed trade
  Profit Factor     Sum of winning R / sum of losing R
  Net Points        Total P&L in index points across all closed trades
  Net by Month      Points per calendar month (YYYY-MM grid)

R-MULTIPLE CALCULATION:
  Uses exit price vs entry price vs rDist (Inside zone range).
  Example: rDist=50, entry=21500, exit at TP1=21525 → pnlPts=25, R=+0.50
  SL hit: exit=21450 → pnlPts=-50, R=-1.00

NOTE: Trade store is in-memory — it resets when the server restarts.
      This is a known limitation. Persistent storage (SQLite or flat-file)
      is a future build item. Until then, log outcomes in the Trades tab
      BEFORE restarting the server if you want the analytics to reflect them.
